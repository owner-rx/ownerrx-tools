# Pedro Valera Digital Brand Studio

A branded content creation system that ensures every presentation, PDF, pitch deck, and document follows your Pedro Valera Digital brand identity and uses proven storytelling and marketing psychology to connect with your audience.

## What's Inside

### Skills

- **Brand Identity** — Your complete brand guidelines: colors (#0A0F1C, #2563EB, #22C55E, etc.), typography (Montserrat + Inter), visual style rules, and deliverable-specific design specs.
- **Storytelling Framework** — The custom IGNITE framework (Identify → Gauge → Narrate → Introduce → Trust → Empower) plus advanced persuasion techniques tailored for entrepreneurial audiences.

### Commands

| Command | What it does |
|---------|-------------|
| `/keynote [topic]` | Create a keynote presentation (12-20 slides) |
| `/workshop [topic]` | Create a workshop presentation with exercises (15-30 slides) |
| `/pdf-guide [topic]` | Create a branded PDF guide or workbook |
| `/pitch-deck [topic]` | Create a pitch deck (10-15 slides) |
| `/one-pager [topic]` | Create a branded single-page summary |

## Usage

Just type a command with your topic:

- `/keynote The Future of Independent Pharmacy`
- `/workshop Building Your First Revenue Dashboard`
- `/pdf-guide 5 Steps to Pharmacy Automation`
- `/pitch-deck Pedro Valera Digital Platform for Investors`
- `/one-pager Pedro Valera Digital Service Overview`

Every piece of content will automatically use your brand colors, fonts, and the IGNITE storytelling structure.

## Customization

To update brand colors, fonts, or style rules, edit the files in `skills/brand-identity/`. To adjust the storytelling framework, edit `skills/storytelling-framework/`.
